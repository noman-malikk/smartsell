Your privacy

Privacy Policy

SellSmart Tools UK is designed to work without accounts, databases, or server-side tool processing.

Information entered into tools

Figures, titles, dimensions, and weights entered into the current tools are processed locally in your browser. They are not sent to a SellSmart Tools UK database.

Do not enter sensitive personal, financial, customer, or account information into any tool.

Accounts and storage

No account or login is required. The current website has no user database and does not provide server-side file uploads.

The tools do not intentionally save your entries after you leave or refresh the page.

Analytics, advertising, and cookies

The MVP does not include external analytics, advertising scripts, or ad tracking. If analytics, advertising, affiliate tools, or cookies are added later, this policy will be updated before or when those services are introduced.

Contact

There is no active website contact form. The contact page uses an email link, which opens your chosen email service or application.

Emails are handled by the sender's and recipient's email providers, not by the browser tools on this website.
