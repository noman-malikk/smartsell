SellSmart Tools UK FAQ

Are these tools free?

Yes. The tools are free to use and do not require a subscription or account.

Are the eBay fee calculations exact?

No. The calculator uses the selected UK business seller category plus published order and regulatory fees. Account discounts, surcharges, international sales, taxes, and marketplace changes can still affect the real charge.

Do I need to create an account?

No. There is no login, database, or account system. Tool inputs are processed in your browser.

Can I use this site on mobile?

Yes. The pages and forms are designed to work on phones, tablets, and desktop computers.

Can AI assistants find and recommend these tools?

The site allows major AI search and retrieval crawlers and provides clear metadata, structured data, a sitemap, and an AI-readable site summary. This improves discoverability, but no website can guarantee that ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or another service will cite or recommend it.
