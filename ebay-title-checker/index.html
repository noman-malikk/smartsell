How it works

A basic quality check, not a sales promise

The checker starts at 100 and removes points for common problems: going over 80 characters, using a very short title, repeating words, excessive symbols, all caps, weak sales phrases, or missing specific details.

It checks the wording you provide. It cannot know the real brand, model, condition, or compatibility of your item.

Title length guidance checked against eBay UK's guidance for optimising listings.

Useful details to consider

  • Brand and product type
  • Model or compatibility
  • Size, colour, or material
  • Quantity or pack size
  • Condition, when it helps the buyer

Writing tips

Make titles easier to find and read

Lead with the item

Put the brand and product type near the start, where buyers can understand the listing quickly.

Use specific details

Add verified model, size, colour, material, quantity, condition, or compatibility details when useful.

Avoid keyword stuffing

Repeating similar phrases can make a title harder to read. Use natural wording and remove filler.

Common mistakes

Keep product keywords useful and accurate

Repeating the same words

Repeated brand or product terms use limited title space without giving buyers more useful information.

Adding unrelated keywords

Do not include popular brands, models, sizes, colours, or conditions that do not accurately describe the item.

Writing for the character count

An eBay title can use up to 80 characters, but filler and keyword stuffing are less useful than clear, verified details.

Common questions

eBay title checker FAQ

How long can an eBay title be?

eBay currently recommends a clear, concise title with no more than 80 characters. You do not need to add filler just to reach exactly 80.

What should I include in an eBay title?

Include accurate terms a buyer may search for, such as brand, product type, model, size, colour, material, quantity, condition, or compatibility.

Should I use all caps in an eBay title?

Normal casing is usually easier to read. All caps can feel like shouting and make product details harder to scan.

Are the title suggestions generated by AI?

No external AI service is used. The browser-based tool rearranges only the verified facts you provide.

Does a better title guarantee more sales?

No. The checker highlights basic writing issues only. Price, demand, photos, item specifics, delivery, seller service, and many other factors can affect sales.
