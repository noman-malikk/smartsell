Your privacy

Privacy Policy

SellSmart Tools UK is designed to work without accounts, databases, or server-side tool processing.

Information entered into tools

Calculator figures, parcel dimensions, parcel weights, and eBay titles entered into the current tools are processed locally in your browser. They are not sent to or stored in a SellSmart Tools UK database or server.

Do not enter sensitive personal, financial, customer, or account information into any tool.

Accounts and storage

No account or login is required. The website has no login system, user database, or server-side file uploads.

The tools do not intentionally save your entries after you leave or refresh the page.

Analytics, advertising, and cookies

Google Analytics may be added later to understand general site usage. Analytics will only load after a visitor actively accepts analytics cookies and a real Google Analytics measurement ID has been configured.

You can reject analytics. Rejecting does not prevent you from using the tools. The current preference is saved in your browser's local storage so the banner does not appear on every page.

No analytics preference has been saved.

Contact

Contact is currently available through an email link only. There is no active website contact form.

Emails are handled by the sender's and recipient's email providers, not by the browser tools on this website.
